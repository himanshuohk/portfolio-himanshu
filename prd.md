# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## Project Name
Personal Portfolio Website – Himanshu Raj

---

# Vision
Create a premium personal portfolio website that positions Himanshu Raj as an AI-native business operator rather than a traditional student, freelancer, marketer, or developer.

The website should communicate:
* Business understanding
* Execution capability
* AI adoption mindset
* Operational thinking
* Entrepreneurial ambition
* Real-world project experience

The objective is to attract:
* Startup founders
* Internship opportunities
* Product and operations roles
* AI implementation opportunities
* Strategic collaborations

---

# Positioning Statement
Himanshu Raj is a BBA student who combines business thinking, digital execution, AI-assisted workflows, website systems, project coordination, and entrepreneurial experimentation.

His value lies in understanding business problems and leveraging modern technology and AI tools to create practical solutions.

---

# Core Brand Archetype
Primary Archetype: Builder
Secondary Archetype: Operator
Supporting Archetype: Explorer

The website should make visitors feel:
* Ambitious
* Curious
* Forward-thinking
* Business-focused
* AI-native

# Website Objectives
1. Establish credibility.
2. Showcase real projects.
3. Demonstrate learning ability.
4. Highlight business thinking.
5. Present a clear growth trajectory.
6. Differentiate from generic BBA students.
7. Create trust through documented work.
